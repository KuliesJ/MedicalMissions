Seeking to Help the Poor

Seeking to help the poor, and to create life conditions according to human rights and dignity, the Christian Life Movement USA, and Solidaridad en Marcha (SEM) are working to provide medical care to the poorest of the poor in Peru and Latin America. Many of these children and families suffer from serious infections and diseases. In the past, our Medical Missions have aided approximately 3,500 patients per trip in an average of 3 days.
