About the Medical Missions to Peru

The Medical Missions to Peru are a joint effort of Solidaridad en Marcha (SEM) with a large group of volunteers from North America and Latin America. In some trips, we join efforts with the Canadian organization Solidarity Experiences Abroad, Inc. Our missions seek to help people in material and spiritual need, according to their dignity and human rights.

About Solidaridad en Marcha (SEM)

Solidaridad en Marcha (SEM) is an association of the Christian Life Movement USA (CLM-USA), which for 22 years has helped to sponsor integral human development in most needed Peruvian communities through the promotion of health, education, and community development.
